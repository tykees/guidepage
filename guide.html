Typeearn is a multi-use application built on the blockchain technology to boost learning and encourage typing activities to generate rewards. Content creators and curators also have a share in the rewards system.
A blockchain is a digital record of transactions. A chain of transactions, in which individual records, called blocks, are linked in a single list. Blockchain aims to make easy and eliminate third parties in transactions. People call it a disrupting technology because it is changing every industry.
Neo is an open-source, community driven platform that is leveraging the intrinsic advantages of blockchain technology to realize the optimized digital world of the future.
Typeearn(TPN) is a NEP-5 token built on the NEO blockchain TPN is the token used in rewarding miners, users, and everyone using the TypeEarn ecosystem. NEP-5 is the standard for tokens to run on the NEO blockchain and NEP stands for NEO Enhancement Proposals.
There are several ways of getting rewards on TypeEarn, but these are the primary:
  • Publish contents
  • Like contents
  • Engage in typing.
Vim credit is the power to use the Typeearn ecosystem. Activities such as publishing, voting (like/dislike), and typing consume your Vim credits and recharge back within some time, max, 5days. The rate of recharge is based on user Confer-share and gen percentage per minute, all based on stake.
Badges are unique elements for users. The badges on TypeEarn are Ostrich, Penguin, Swan, Bustard, Pelican and Flamingo, which is the lowest.
